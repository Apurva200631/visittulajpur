Welcome to Tuljapur

Tuljapur is one of the most sacred pilgrimage towns in Maharashtra. The town is famous for the Tulja Bhavani Temple, one of the important Shakti Peeths. Devotees from across India visit Tuljapur every year to seek blessings of Goddess Bhavani.
