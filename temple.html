About the Temple

Tulja Bhavani Temple is one of the most important temples in Maharashtra. The temple is located on the Balaghat mountain range in Tuljapur. The main deity is Goddess Bhavani, worshipped as a powerful protector goddess.

Temple Timings

  • Morning Darshan: 4:00 AM – 12:00 PM
  • Afternoon Darshan: 2:00 PM – 6:00 PM
  • Evening Aarti: 7:00 PM

Temple Rituals

Daily rituals include Kakad Aarti, Abhishek, and evening Aarti. Special ceremonies are performed during Navratri.
